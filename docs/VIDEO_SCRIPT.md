## RECORDING TIPS

1. **Resolution:** 1920x1080 minimum
2. **Browser:** Chrome with Phantom extension
3. **Wallet:** Pre-funded on Devnet
4. **Network:** Switch Phantom to Devnet BEFORE recording

7. **Cursor:** Make movements deliberate, not jittery
